    HOW TO PLAY:

  • Gollum will be assigned hit points at start of each round
  • There are 4 characters chosen for you and each character gets different attack points at start of each round
  • By selecting (clicking) a character their attack point will be added to Fellowship's points

    • HOW TO WIN:

  • The Fellowship will win the round by matching total points against Gollum
  • The Fellowship will loose the round if the points go above Gollum's
  • Choose carefully and have a good game!!
